Dr. Matthew Walker, a neuroscientist and sleep expert, delves into the science of sleep and its critical role in memory, creativity, health, and happiness. The book discusses the dangers of sleep deprivation, the benefits of REM and non-REM sleep, and practical advice on improving sleep quality. It aims to educate readers on why sleep is essential for a healthier, longer, and more fulfilling life.
