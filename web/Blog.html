    My Top 3 Favourite Dishes

    In this world, there are so many delicious dishes. These are the three dishes I love the most. Of course, other dishes are also delicious in their own unique way. 

    Rice beef bowl

    Thin juicy slices of USDA choice beef simmered with fresh onions in a special sauce of herbs and spices over a bed of plump steamed white rice.

    Grilled Pork Jow

    Pork jowl is an underrated cut that is flavourful, fatty, tender, juicy...perfect for the grill. It's popular cut for the barbecue in Thailand, and the nam jim jeaw dipping sauce is a classic pairing!

    Rad Na Mee Krob

    Crispy Fried Noodles with tender pork or Rad Na Mee Krob, is one of a popular Thai street food meal.
